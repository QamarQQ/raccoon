SwitchingFunctionPenalty

Penalty kernel to constrain the sum of all switching functions in a multiphase system.

The penalty is added in the form of a free energy term

where is the penalty prefactor (penalty) and is the constraint. The constraint is enforced approximately to a tolerance of (depending on the shape and units of the free energy).
